Session 3.2: Building an Intelligent Knowledge Base (No-Code RAG)

Let's ask a question that was impossible yesterday: What if you could "talk" to your entire team's history of CVs and interview notes? Today, we're going to build the tool that does exactly that.

What is RAG? (The Simple Explanation)

It stands for Retrieval-Augmented Generation, and it sounds complicated, but the concept is simple.

"RAG is the process of giving a brilliant expert (the AI) your private library of documents to read before they answer your question."

This ensures the answers are grounded in your data, not the public internet. It's secure, relevant, and incredibly powerful.

The Power of RAG: Meet Your "CV & Candidate Info Bot"

This is what our final product will be able to do. Imagine asking a chat bot these questions about your team's private CV database:

  • "Which candidates have experience with both Python and AWS?"
  • "Summarize Jane Doe's experience and highlight her top 3 skills."
  • "Who is the most experienced project manager for a fintech role based in London?"

Today's Project: Build Your Bot

This is a guided, hands-on lab. We will walk through every step together to create your first AI application.

Part 1: The Build (20 Mins)

We'll use a no-code tool (like Vertex AI Search) to create a secure "Data Store" and upload a provided set of 20-30 anonymized sample CVs. The system will then "read" and "index" this information.

Part 2: The Test (40 Mins)

Once indexing is complete, you'll get access to the chat interface for your bot. Your task is to complete the "Scavenger Hunt" to test its capabilities.

Lab Task: The Scavenger Hunt

Use the chat interface for the bot you just built to find the answers to these questions:

  1. Find all candidates with a PMP certification.
  2. Which candidates mention "data analysis" as a skill?
  3. Summarize the CV of the candidate named "John Smith".
  4. Who has more years of experience in management, "Jane Doe" or "Peter Jones"?
  5. Which candidate is the best fit for a role requiring experience in both marketing and e-commerce? Explain why.

Key Takeaways & Next Steps

Your new superpower is that you have just built one of the most powerful tools in modern AI: a secure application that can reason over your team's collective knowledge. The golden rule is that the power of RAG is its ability to ground AI in trusted, specific data.
