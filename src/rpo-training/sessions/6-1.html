Session 6.1: Developing an RPO-Specific AI Roadmap

A Strategic Workshop for RPO Leadership. Our goal is to collaboratively build the strategic map that will guide our team's AI efforts for the next year, ensuring we invest in projects that deliver maximum business value.

The Strategic Foundation

Connecting Capabilities to Business Goals

1. Our Team's Proven Capabilities

We'll review the successful projects from the Phase 2 "AI Champions" showcase (e.g., the CV Bot, automated workflows). These are the tools we know we can build.

2. Our Department's Business Objectives

We'll review our key goals for the year (e.g., reduce time-to-fill by 15%, improve source-of-hire quality, etc.).

The Key Question for Today: "How can we apply our proven AI capabilities to directly accelerate our most important business objectives?"

Workshop Part 1: Opportunity Mapping

Brainstorming Across the Recruiting Lifecycle

The Goal: To create a comprehensive list of all potential AI projects.

The Activity: Using a digital whiteboard, we will map out the entire RPO lifecycle:

Intake → Sourcing → Screening → Submitting → Scheduling → Onboarding

For each stage, we will brainstorm answers to two questions:

  1. What are our biggest pain points or bottlenecks in this stage?
  2. How could an AI tool or automation help solve them?

The Prioritization Tool: The Impact vs. Effort Matrix

Not all ideas are created equal. We will use this simple but powerful matrix to prioritize our list and spark crucial strategic conversations about value versus resources.

Quick Wins

(High Impact / Low Effort)

Do these now!

Major Projects

(High Impact / High Effort)

Plan for these.

Fill-ins

(Low Impact / Low Effort)

Consider these later.

Thankless Tasks

(Low Impact / High Effort)

Avoid these.

From a Map to a Plan

Workshop Part 2: Prioritizing

The Goal: To create a prioritized visual map of all brainstormed ideas.

As a leadership team, we will collectively discuss each idea from the Opportunity Mapping exercise and place it onto the Impact/Effort matrix.

Workshop Part 3: Building the Roadmap

The Goal: To select our top projects and create a clear, sequenced plan of action.

We will identify the top 3-5 initiatives from the "Quick Wins" and "Major Projects" quadrants and sequence them on a simple timeline (e.g., Q3, Q4, Next Q1).

Key Takeaways & Next Steps

What We've Achieved Today:

  • A shared vision for AI in our department.
  • A comprehensive list of potential opportunities.
  • A prioritized list of our top 3-5 projects.
  • A draft roadmap for the next 6-9 months.

This is our strategic foundation.
