Lab Activities

Lab 1: The Data Janitor (20 Mins)

Your Goal: Clean and structure a messy, real-world-style dataset using only natural language prompts.

  • Open the sample Google Sheet provided.
  • Use Gemini prompts to clean at least three different columns.
  • Create one new column by extracting information from another.
  • Be ready to share your most effective cleaning prompt!

Lab 2: The Data Detective (20 Mins)

Your Goal: To answer three specific business questions from your newly cleaned dataset using only natural language prompts.

  • What is the average time-to-fill for roles in Texas vs. California?
  • Who are the top 3 candidate sources that lead to hires?
  • Create a bar chart showing the number of hires per month for the last 6 months.

Key Takeaways & Next Steps

Your new superpower is the ability to turn any spreadsheet into an interactive dashboard. The golden rule is that clean data is the foundation for good insights. Use AI to automate the cleaning so you can focus on the analysis.
