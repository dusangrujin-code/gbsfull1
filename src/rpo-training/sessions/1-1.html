Session 1.1: Prompt Engineering 101 for Recruiters

Your guide to moving from simple questions to expert commands.

The #1 Skill for Working with AI

The goal today is to become an expert instructor for your AI assistant. In our survey, the #1 skill gap was knowing how to get the AI to do what you actually want.

Think of AI as the world's most brilliant junior assistant: it's incredibly fast and knowledgeable, but it has zero context about your job. A vague instruction gets a vague result. An expert instruction gets an expert result.

A Tale of Two Prompts

The "Before" Prompt

This is how most people start. The result is generic, boring, and won't attract top talent because the AI has to guess everything.

write a job description for a software engineer

The "After" Prompt

This is an expert command. It gives the AI a professional brief, not a vague question, leading to a high-quality result.

[ROLE] You are an expert RPO sourcer...
[CONTEXT] We are hiring a "Senior Full-Stack Engineer"...
[TASK] Write a compelling job description...
[FORMAT] Structure the output with sections...

The R.C.T.F.E. Framework

An expert prompt contains five key ingredients. Mastering this structure is the key to getting high-quality results every time.

Role

Who is the AI?

Context

What's the background?

Task

What should it do?

Format

How should it look?

Exemplar

What's a good example?

Lab Activities

Lab 1: Creating a Candidate Persona (15 Mins)

Your goal is to create a detailed candidate persona for a hard-to-fill "Senior Clinical Research Associate" role. If you don't know who you're looking for, you can't write content that resonates. Use the R.C.T.F. structure to build your prompt.

View Example Persona Prompt
[ROLE] You are a senior life sciences recruiter...
[CONTEXT] Our client is a global pharma leader looking to hire a Senior CRA based in Budapest...
[TASK] Create a detailed candidate persona for this ideal individual.
[FORMAT] Structure the output using these exact headings: Summary, Demographics & Experience, Key Skills, Motivations, Where to Find Them...

Lab 2: Drafting a Compelling Job Description (20 Mins)

Now, use the persona you just created to draft a job description that speaks directly to them. This time, add the "E" for Exemplar to show the AI the exact tone of voice you want.

View Example Job Description Prompt
[ROLE] You are an expert recruitment marketing copywriter...
[CONTEXT] We are writing a job description for a Senior CRA role... We need to attract the candidate from the persona we just created...
[TASK] Write a full job description for this role...
[FORMAT] Structure the output with these headings: "Your Mission," "A Day in the Life,"...
[EXEMPLAR - TONE OF VOICE] Use a tone similar to this: "Join us in a role that goes beyond the ordinary..."

Wrap-Up & Next Steps

Your new superpower is the R.C.T.F.E. Framework. Remember the golden rule: Vague Instructions = Vague Results. Expert Instructions = Expert Results.
