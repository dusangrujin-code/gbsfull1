Session 1.2: AI-Powered Email & Content Lab

Our Goal: To move beyond just drafting. Today, you will learn to use AI as your co-editor, creative partner, and summarizer for the most important writing you do: candidate outreach. We will learn to write better emails, faster.

Go Beyond the First Draft

Think of the time spent perfecting an email's tone. Is it too formal? Too casual? Too long? AI is your secret weapon for refinement. You can use it for three key tasks:

  • Tone & Style Modulation: Instantly change the feel of your message.
  • Summarization: Condense long documents into punchy, effective emails.
  • A/B Testing: Create variations to see what gets the best response.

Example: Changing Your Tone

Start with a standard sentence:

"We have an opening for a Senior Clinical Research Associate that I believe aligns with your professional background."

Prompt 1: Make it more engaging

Rewrite this sentence to be more energetic and direct...
Result: "I came across your profile and was immediately impressed... I'm hiring for a Senior CRA role that seems like a perfect match..."

Prompt 2: Make it more formal

Rewrite this for a highly senior, executive-level candidate...
Result: "Given your distinguished career... I am writing to introduce a Senior CRA opportunity that I believe may be of strategic interest to you."

Example: The Power of Summarization

Don't manually summarize a long job description ever again. Give the AI the document and tell it what you need. This is a massive time-saver.
